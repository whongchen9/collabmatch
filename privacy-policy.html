🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2026 | Last Updated: June 9, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how CollabMatch ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our collaborative matching platform at https://cloudbase-d6g8yog0ub3e56efe-1427257718.tcloudbaseapp.com (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address — when you register or log in via email
  • Phone number — when you log in via SMS verification code
  • Nickname — displayed as your public profile name
  • Avatar — automatically generated from the first character of your nickname

2.2 Profile & Collaboration Data

  • Skills — tags you add to your profile (e.g., React, Python, Figma)
  • Domain preference — your chosen collaboration field (Tech, Design, Content, Education, Business, Campus)
  • Position / Role — your self-described role
  • Requirements you post — project descriptions, timelines, collaboration mode
  • Applications & group messages — your interactions with other users

2.3 Technical & Usage Data

  • Authentication token — stored locally in your browser's localStorage (not sent to our servers beyond API authentication)
  • IP address & browser type — automatically collected by our cloud infrastructure for security and analytics
  • AI conversation logs — if you use the CollabAI assistant, your dialogue content is processed by our AI provider (Volcano Ark / Doubao API) and may be temporarily stored for service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core matching service — match you with suitable collaborators based on skills, domain, and project needs
  • To enable communication — display your profile to potential collaborators, deliver in-app messages
  • To improve our AI matching — analyze skill tags and project descriptions to refine match accuracy
  • To ensure platform security — detect fraudulent behavior, prevent spam
  • To comply with legal obligations — retain logs as required by applicable law

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent — when you register an account and agree to this Policy
  • Contractual necessity — to provide the matching service you requested
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our service, prevent fraud, and ensure security

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Volcano Ark (Doubao API) — processes AI chat content; subject to their privacy policy at https://www.volcengine.com/docs
  • Tencent CloudBase — our cloud infrastructure provider; data hosted on Tencent Cloud servers
  • Other users — your public profile (nickname, skills, domain) is visible to other users for collaboration matching

6. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Tencent Cloud servers located in Mainland China. If you access the Service from the EU, UK, or other regions with cross-border data transfer restrictions, you acknowledge that your data will be transferred to and processed in China. We will take appropriate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with applicable law.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts — data retained while your account is active
  • Inactive accounts — data may be anonymized or deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • Logs — security and access logs retained for 6 months as required by Chinese law

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your account and data
  • Right to data portability — export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object / restrict processing
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can delete your account at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: collabmatch@example.com

9. Cookies & Local Storage

  • We use localStorage to store your authentication token (collabmatch_token)
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies
  • You can clear localStorage via your browser settings; this will log you out

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

11. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

When you use the CollabAI assistant, the responses are generated by artificial intelligence. We disclose this clearly within the chat interface. AI-generated content is provided for reference only; you are responsible for reviewing and verifying any information before relying on it.

12. Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Authentication uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) with a 7-day expiration
  • All API communication is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Database access is restricted by role-based security rules

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice on the Service or sending an in-app message. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
